Decision Guide: Which Approach Is Right?

Use Landing Page Builders If:

You're testing a new offer or market
Launch fast, validate demand, iterate based on data. No long-term commitment needed.
Your budget is under $500/month
Builders scale from $50-300/month; specialists start at $3K+
You need to launch in days, not weeks
Builders: 1-2 hours. Specialists: 2-3 weeks. Custom: 4-8 weeks.
You're okay with template-based design
Builders use proven templates. Results are good; differentiation is limited.
You want to manage pages yourself
Builders are designed for non-technical users. Full control, no coding.

Hire a Specialist If:

Conversion rate is a key metric
Specialists run experiments, optimize copy, test design variations. 15-30% lift is typical.
You're running 10+ campaigns
Portfolio scale justifies $3K-8K/month cost. Per-page cost drops significantly.
You need ongoing optimization
Builders are "set and forget." Specialists monitor, test, and improve continuously.
Your brand needs custom design
Template limitations hurt your brand. Custom design builds differentiation.
You have complex integrations
Complex CRM flows, custom data requirements. Builders have limits.

Build Custom If:

You have $5K-20K+ budget for build
One-time investment for full ownership and unlimited customization.
You're building a complete funnel system
Multiple pages, complex user flows. Custom build scales better long-term.
You need proprietary functionality
Advanced features, custom integrations, unique user experience.
You want zero platform dependency
Full control. No vendor lock-in. You own the code and hosting.

Common Questions

What conversion rate should I expect? +
Builders: 1-3% (average). Specialists: 3-8% (optimized). Custom: 5-15%+ (fully optimized). Rates depend on offer, traffic quality, and industry.
Can I move pages between platforms? +
Difficult. Builder pages are locked in. Specialist pages are usually custom. Custom development is portable. Plan for this when choosing.
How long does it take to see results? +
Builders: 1-2 weeks (if traffic is steady). Specialists: 4-8 weeks (testing phase). Custom: 6-12 weeks (optimization phase). Patience pays off.
What if I outgrow my platform? +
Start with builders to validate. Move to specialists when you have product-market fit. Migrate to custom when you're scaling 10+ pages/month.
Can I use multiple platforms simultaneously? +
Yes, many smart marketers do. Use builders for quick tests, specialists for your core funnel, custom for proprietary features.

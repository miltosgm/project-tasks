Common Questions

Is BacklinkU's data as accurate as Ahrefs?

Ahrefs has a larger link index (43 trillion links vs our growing database). For most small businesses checking their own backlinks and competitors, BacklinkU provides sufficient accuracy. If you need the absolute most comprehensive data available, Ahrefs wins.

Can I switch from Ahrefs to BacklinkU?

Yes, if you're primarily using Ahrefs for backlink analysis. If you rely heavily on keyword research, site audits, or rank tracking, you'll need additional tools to replace those features.

Does BacklinkU plan to add more features?

We're focused on being the best backlink checker, not an all-in-one SEO suite. We'll add features that enhance backlink analysis, but we won't bloat the product with unrelated tools.

What's the catch with the free tier?

No catch. Limited searches per day, but full feature access. It's designed for people who check backlinks occasionally. If you need it daily, upgrade to paid.

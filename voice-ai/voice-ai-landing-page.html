FAQ

Common questions.

Will patients know it's an AI calling them? +

With voice cloning, patients hear your actual receptionist's voice — not a generic robot. Many of our clients report patients saying "thanks for calling personally." That said, in an abundance of caution (and for compliance), the call can include a brief disclosure that it's an automated reminder.

Is VoiceDesk HIPAA compliant? +

Yes. VoiceDesk runs on ElevenLabs (ISO 27001 certified) and Twilio (HIPAA-eligible infrastructure). We do not store patient health information on our servers. We provide a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) as part of the setup. Your compliance team will be satisfied.

What practice management software does it work with? +

VoiceDesk integrates with Dentrix, Open Dental, and Eaglesoft via webhook. Other systems can connect via our API or through Zapier/Make.com integrations. If you're on something else, contact us — we can usually get you connected within a day.

How long does setup take? +

Most practices are live in under an hour. You'll record a 2-minute voice sample, connect your scheduling software, and customize your reminder script. We handle everything else. If you're on the Scale plan, we do the setup with you in a 1:1 onboarding call.

What if a patient doesn't pick up? +

VoiceDesk leaves a voicemail and then automatically sends an SMS reminder as a fallback. If the appointment is still unconfirmed 4 hours before, it sends a final text nudge. Nothing falls through the cracks.

Can I customize what the AI says? +

Absolutely. You can edit the reminder script, add specific instructions (e.g., "bring your insurance card"), adjust the tone, and set different scripts for different appointment types. Growth and Scale plans include a full script builder.

Is there a contract? +

No contracts, ever. VoiceDesk is month-to-month. You can cancel anytime with no penalties. We also offer a 30-day free trial so you can see the no-show impact before you spend a cent.

What's included in the free trial? +

Your free 30-day trial includes everything in the Growth plan — up to 300 reminder calls, voice cloning, 2-way IVR, and real-time dashboard. No credit card required to start. After 30 days, you choose your plan or walk away with no charge.
